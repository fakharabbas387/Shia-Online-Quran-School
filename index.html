Nazra Quran

Nazra Quran is the practice of reading the Holy Quran in Arabic with correct pronunciation (Tajweed) without translation. It is important for both kids and adults.

Tajweed Quran

Tajweed Quran means reciting the Holy Quran with proper rules of pronunciation and articulation. It teaches kids and adults how to read each Arabic letter correctly.

Tafseer Quran

Tafseer Quran is the explanation and interpretation of the Holy Quran that helps people understand its true meanings and messages.

Nahjul Balagha

Nahjul Balagha is a famous collection
of sermons, letters, and sayings of Ali ibn Abi Talib, the cousin and son-in-law of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). It was compiled by Al-Sharif al-Radi in the 10th century
